Guide

Plan and prioritize your shiny hunts with the Hunting List feature!

How it works:

  • Use the search bar to quickly find the Pokémon you want. Only Pokémon available in the game will appear in the list.
  • Drag and drop Pokémon icons from the right side into the list on the left to build your hunting queue.
  • Organize your targets by priority by reordering them in the list.
  • Each Pokémon in your list will show the best route or location where it can be found.
  • To remove a Pokémon from your list, simply drag it back to the right.

This tool helps you stay focused and makes your hunting sessions faster and more efficient!
