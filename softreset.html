Welcome to the Soft Reset Counter by Krul!

This tool helps you track your soft reset attempts while hunting for shiny or specific Pokémon.

How to Use:

  • Choose the Pokémon you want to hunt and save your game in front of it.
  • Start an encounter with the Pokémon.
  • After the encounter, click the button to increase the counter by one.
  • If it's not the result you want, reset the game and repeat the process.
