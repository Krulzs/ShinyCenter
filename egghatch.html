Guide

Ready to start your Egg hunt? Here's how to use the Egg Hatcher Counter:

Steps:

  • Head to Nightingale Town.
  • Leave the two Pokémon you want to breed at the Daycare.
  • Collect eggs from the Old Man standing in front of the building.
  • Each time you hatch an egg, click to increment the counter and keep track of your progress!

Good Luck!

Do you really want to reset the counter?

Use a Pokémon with Flame Body, Magma Armor, or Steam Engine in your party to hatch eggs twice as fast.
